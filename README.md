Mead Map is intended to be a web-based mapping app to showcase meaderies and producers of mead (i.e. wineries or distilleries that also produce mead). 
Mead (honey wine) is one of the oldest forms of alcohol, but it is often overshadowed by beer, grape wine, and other spirits in marketing and competitions.
This app will primarily be tailored to mead enthusiasts, content creators, and service industry professionals who are looking for mead nearby.
